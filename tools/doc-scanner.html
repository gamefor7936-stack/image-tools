Zero-Trust AI Document Scanner

Transform any smartphone photo into a perfectly flat, clean, and professional PDF document. Unlike commercial scanning apps that upload your sensitive ID cards, receipts, or legal contracts to the cloud, the MARK_Tools Doc Scanner Pro runs complex Computer Vision algorithms (OpenCV) directly inside your browser. Your images never leave your device.

Smart Deskew (Perspective Correction)

Took a photo of a document from a weird angle? No problem. By dragging the four blue anchor points to the corners of your paper, our engine uses a mathematical perspective transform matrix to flatten and straighten the image perfectly as if it was scanned flat.

Adaptive Thresholding Filters

Remove unwanted shadows and uneven lighting. The B&W Document filter applies an adaptive block threshold to make text deep black and backgrounds pure white. Adjust the "Strength" slider to fine-tune the noise reduction algorithm.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I scan multiple pages into one PDF?

Yes! Simply click "Add Page" on the sidebar to upload another photo. The system will create a queue. Once you've cropped and applied filters to all of them, clicking "Save PDF" will bind them all into a single, multi-page document.

Why does the browser freeze slightly when applying a filter?

Applying advanced image processing (like grayscale conversion, Gaussian blur, and thresholding) to high-resolution smartphone photos requires significant CPU power. Because we prioritize your privacy by doing this locally, your browser is doing heavy mathematical lifting.
