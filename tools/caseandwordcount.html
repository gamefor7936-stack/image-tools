Frequently Asked Questions

What is Title Case vs Sentence Case?

Title Case capitalizes the first letter of Every Single Word (often used for Blog Titles or YouTube Thumbnails). Sentence case only capitalizes the first letter of the first word in a sentence, mirroring natural writing styles.

How is Reading Time calculated?

The estimated reading time is based on the universally accepted average reading speed of adult humans, which is roughly 200 to 250 words per minute. Our tool divides your total word count by 200 and rounds up to the nearest minute.
