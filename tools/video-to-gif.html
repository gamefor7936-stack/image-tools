What is Video to GIF Converter?

Video to GIF Converter transforms MP4 and WebM videos into animated GIFs directly in your browser. No uploads required - all processing happens locally for maximum privacy and speed. Perfect for creating shareable content.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Select Video: Choose MP4 or WebM file from your device.
  2. Adjust Settings: Set quality, frame rate, and dimensions.
  3. Convert: Click convert to process the video.
  4. Download: Save the animated GIF to your device.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What video formats are supported?

MP4 and WebM formats are supported. For best results, use H.264 encoded MP4 files with standard frame rates.

How does quality affect the GIF?

Higher quality preserves more detail but creates larger files. Lower quality reduces file size but may lose sharpness.

Why is local processing important?

No video uploads means your content stays private. Processing in your browser ensures security and works offline.
