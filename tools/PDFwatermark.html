What is PDF Watermark Pro?

PDF Watermark Pro adds professional text or logo watermarks to your PDF documents with precise positioning and live preview. Use it for document protection, branding, or marking confidential materials with customizable colors, opacity, and positioning.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Upload PDF: Select your PDF document to watermark.
  2. Add Watermark: Enter text or upload a logo image.
  3. Customize: Adjust color, opacity, size, and position using the interactive canvas.
  4. Apply: Click "Apply Watermark" to process the document.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do opacity levels affect the watermark?

Lower opacity creates subtle watermarks, while higher opacity makes them more prominent. Use 10-30% for professional documents.

Can I add multiple watermarks?

Yes, you can add multiple text or logo watermarks to the same document by repeating the process or using different layers.

How precise is the positioning?

The interactive canvas allows pixel-perfect positioning with drag, rotate, and scale controls for professional results.
