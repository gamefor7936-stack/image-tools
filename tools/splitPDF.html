Client-Side PDF Splitter & Extractor

Working with massive PDF documents can be cumbersome, especially when you only need to send a specific section via email or upload a single page to a portal. The MARK_Tools PDF Splitter allows you to effortlessly extract individual pages or divide huge documents into fixed-size chunks. Best of all, it packages all your newly split files into a single, organized .ZIP archive for a seamless download experience.

100% Secure & Privacy First

Most online PDF tools force you to upload your sensitive documents (like invoices, legal contracts, or medical records) to their remote servers for processing. We don't. By utilizing modern WebAssembly and JavaScript algorithms, the entire splitting process happens locally inside your device's memory (RAM). Your files never touch the internet, guaranteeing zero risk of data leaks.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the system output a ZIP file?

Browsers restrict websites from downloading dozens of files simultaneously to prevent spam and freezing. By dynamically compressing your split PDFs into a single ZIP archive, you get a much faster, one-click download.

Can I split a password-protected PDF?

Currently, browser-based PDF parsers cannot open heavily encrypted (password-protected) documents. You will need to remove the password using an unlocker tool before splitting the file.
