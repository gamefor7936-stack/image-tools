SEO Word Counter & Keyword Density provides comprehensive content analysis for search engine optimization. Track word counts, reading time, and keyword density while filtering out stopwords to identify your most important SEO terms.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are stopwords and why filter them?

Stopwords are common words like "the", "and", "is" that don't contribute to SEO value. Filtering them reveals your actual keyword targets.

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is estimated at 200 words per minute for adults, providing a realistic assessment of content length.

What's the ideal keyword density range?

Aim for 1-3% keyword density. Too low may hurt rankings; too high can appear spammy to search engines.
