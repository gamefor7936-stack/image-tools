Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the HD version sometimes fail to load?

If a video was uploaded in a lower resolution (e.g., 480p or 720p) or is very old, YouTube might not generate a 1080p maxresdefault image for it. In such rare cases, our tool will automatically detect the failure and fallback to the next highest available resolution.

Are downloaded thumbnails copyright-free?

No. Thumbnail graphics are intellectually owned by the video creator or the respective broadcasting network. Downloading them is intended for personal reference, design study, or fair-use editorial commentary. Do not reuse them commercially without explicit permission.
