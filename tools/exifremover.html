What is EXIF Privacy Remover?

EXIF Privacy Remover automatically strips hidden metadata from photos, including GPS coordinates, camera information, and timestamps. Protects your privacy by removing potentially sensitive location and device data before sharing images online.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Upload Photo: Select the image file containing EXIF data.
  2. Process: Click "Remove Metadata & Save" to strip all metadata.
  3. Download: Receive the cleaned image file, safe for sharing.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is EXIF data and why remove it?

EXIF (Exchangeable Image File Format) contains metadata like GPS location, camera model, and timestamps. Removing it protects privacy and prevents location tracking.

What are the privacy implications of EXIF data?

EXIF can reveal your exact location, the device you used, and when photos were taken, potentially compromising personal privacy and security.

How can I verify the metadata has been removed?

Use online EXIF viewers or photo properties to check the cleaned image. All metadata fields should be empty or removed.
