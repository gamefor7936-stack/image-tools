How to Merge PDFs Securely

Combining multiple PDF files shouldn't require installing bulky desktop software or trusting a random server with your highly sensitive documents. The MARK_Tools PDF Merger empowers you to combine reports, invoices, and portfolios directly in your browser. With our intuitive drag-and-drop interface, reordering files takes less than a second.

Zero-Trust Privacy Standard

Unlike traditional cloud converters, we operate on a Zero-Trust architecture. When you click merge, the WebAssembly and JavaScript engines inside your browser instantly read, parse, and stitch your PDFs together locally using your device's RAM. Your financial records, legal contracts, and personal data never touch our servers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to how many files I can merge?

Technically, there is no hard limit. However, because processing happens on your local device, trying to merge 100+ large files simultaneously might consume too much of your computer's RAM. We recommend doing it in batches of 20-30 files for optimal speed.

Does merging compress or reduce PDF quality?

No. Our engine performs a "binary stitch", copying the exact pages from the source files into the new file. There is zero loss in quality, images remain sharp, and text remains vector-based and selectable.
