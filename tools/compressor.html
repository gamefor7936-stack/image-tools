What is Image Compressor?

Image Compressor reduces file sizes of JPEG, PNG, and WebP images while maintaining visual quality. Perfect for web optimization, faster loading times, and reducing storage space without noticeable quality loss.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Upload Image: Select your JPEG, PNG, or WebP image file.
  2. Adjust Quality: Use the slider to balance file size vs. quality.
  3. Compress: Click "Compress & download" to process the image.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How much quality is lost during compression?

Quality loss depends on the slider setting. Higher quality (0.7-1.0) preserves more detail but results in larger files.

Which image formats are supported?

Supports JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. Each format has different compression characteristics and use cases.

Can I compress multiple images at once?

Currently processes one image at a time. For batch compression, process images sequentially or use our bulk tools.
