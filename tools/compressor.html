Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the warning "Size is Larger" appear?

If you upload an image that has already been aggressively optimized (like an image downloaded from Facebook or WhatsApp), trying to compress it again using a standard format wrapper can mathematically increase its file size. The tool warns you so you don't overwrite a perfectly good file.

Are my images saved on your servers?

Absolutely not. This tool operates on a strict Zero-Trust Architecture. You can literally turn off your Wi-Fi, upload an image, and it will still compress perfectly because all the processing code runs locally on your smartphone or computer processor.
