Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At MARK_Tools (accessible from marktoolbox.com), one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by MARK_Tools and how we use it.

1. Local Client-Side Processing (Core Principle)

Unlike traditional online converters, MARK_Tools is built on a Client-Side Architecture. This means:

  • When you use our PDF, Image, or Text tools, your files are processed entirely within your web browser.
  • We DO NOT upload, store, or view your files on our servers.
  • Once you close your browser tab, the processed data is gone permanently.

2. Local Storage & Session Data

To improve your User Experience (UX), we use your browser's LocalStorage and SessionStorage to remember your preferences (like Dark Mode, selected Language, or the last IP address you calculated). This data never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party APIs

Some specific tools (like Global DNS Resolver or MAC Address Lookup) require querying public databases. In such cases, only the specific string you inputted (e.g., a MAC Address or Domain Name) is sent to secure third-party APIs (like Google DNS or MacVendors) to fetch the results. No personal identifying information is transmitted.

4. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display ads on our website to keep our tools 100% free. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies (known as DART cookies) to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet. However, visitors may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy.

5. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.
