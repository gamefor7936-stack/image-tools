Our Story

Started in 2024 by a passionate Computer Systems and Network Engineering student, MARK_Tools was born out of frustration. We noticed that most online tools forced users to upload sensitive documents to unknown servers, subjected them to long waiting queues, or hid basic features behind expensive paywalls.

We decided to change that. By leveraging modern web technologies (like WebAssembly, HTML5 Canvas, and advanced Client-Side JavaScript), we shifted the processing power back to where it belongs: Your Device.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an all-in-one utility suite for professionals, developers, and everyday users worldwide. Whether you are generating a network subnet report, scanning a physical document, or calculating bandwidth, MARK_Tools is your reliable digital Swiss Army Knife.
